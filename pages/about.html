about the company

Welcome to Delicious Dish Tours, where we invite you to embark on a culinary adventure unlike any other. At Delicious Dish Tours, we believe that food is not just sustenance, but a gateway to culture, history, and community.

As avid world travelers turned food tour enthusiasts, we’ve traversed the globe, sampling flavors from bustling street markets in Bangkok to quaint trattorias in Tuscany. Our love for discovering new cuisines and connecting with local communities has led us to create Delicious Dish Tours. With each tour, we aim to share the authentic tastes and cultural richness we’ve experienced during our adventures.

As owners and operators, we bring a wealth of firsthand knowledge and a deep appreciation for culinary diversity to every aspect of our tours. Drawing on our global experiences, we’ve curated a selection of immersive food experiences that highlight the best of local cuisine while providing insights into the stories and traditions behind each dish.

At Delicious Dish Tours, we believe that food is a universal language that transcends borders and brings people together. Whether you’re joining us for a walking tour through food markets or a hands-on cooking class with local chefs, we’re dedicated to creating unforgettable experiences that celebrate the joys of food and travel. Join us on a culinary adventure that will tantalize your taste buds, ignite your curiosity, and leave you with memories to savor long after the journey ends. Welcome to Delicious Dish Tours – where every bite tells a story.

How Delicious Dish Tours Got Started

Delicious Dish Tours began with a simple idea: to celebrate the rich and diverse culinary heritage of Los Angeles while offering an unforgettable experience to food lovers. Founded by passionate travelers and devoted foodies, our journey started with a love for uncovering hidden gems and telling the stories behind each dish.

The inspiration came during a stroll through one of LA’s historic neighborhoods, where vibrant flavors and cultural narratives collided. It was then that we realized the city’s food scene wasn’t just about great taste; it was a gateway to understanding the people, history, and spirit of Los Angeles.

Our Mission

At Delicious Dish Tours, our mission is to inspire connection, curiosity, and joy through the art of food and storytelling. We believe that every meal is a journey, every dish a story, and every neighborhood a treasure trove waiting to be discovered.
Our goal is to provide unforgettable culinary experiences that go beyond the plate. By combining expert knowledge, cultural exploration, and premium service, we aim to create meaningful moments that connect people to the vibrant history and diverse flavors of Los Angeles.
Whether you’re a seasoned traveler, a curious foodie, or someone simply seeking a unique adventure, we’re here to guide you on an extraordinary journey. Together, let’s uncover the hidden gems, savor the unexpected, and share stories that leave lasting memories.

Our Team

Nneka Williams

I’m Nneka (pronounced Neeka), the founder of Delicious Dish Tours. I’m a native of New York City and the daughter of a trained chef and cookbook collector who grew up eating spectacular restaurant quality food at home. As a kid, I developed a taste for a diverse range of cuisines and would try anything put in front of me.

As an adult, I have had the opportunity to travel the world to sample these cuisines in their countries of origin, where they are at their most authentic. The food knowledge I have gained on my travels has influenced my home cooking and has spurred me to build a cookbook collection of my own. I derive great joy from learning and eating, and I have started Delicious Dish Tours in an effort to share my passion for diverse food cultures with others.

My goal is to help curious foodies learn more about the cuisines which exist in the communities which they visit and inspire them to try cooking some of these dishes at home. I also strive to support the restaurant industry, which has been recovering from the difficulties caused by Covid-19. Too many excellent restaurants have recently been forced to close, and my hope is that Delicious Dish Tours will be a positive and consistent source of revenue for the restaurants we visit. My ultimate goal in building this business is to make people happy through food. I welcome tour guests and restaurants to join me on this exciting culinary journey!

Geoff Hardy

I’m Geoff, Nneka’s partner in everything, including Delicious Dish Tours. I grew up in Minnesota, where the traditional local food is not well-known for being full of flavor, but I was the weird kid who wanted to eat all the jalapenos in the nachos at the baseball stadium.
After college, I moved to Boston where I experienced Chinatown and the Italian North End, exploring global food culture for the first time. I was a late bloomer, but now I’ve traveled with Nneka to over 75 countries, and I’m willing to try anything at least once – even insects in Asia and guinea pigs in Peru. I often order the weirdest or the spiciest thing on the menu, and I look forward to helping introduce tour guests to unexpected but delicious foods from our restaurant partners!
