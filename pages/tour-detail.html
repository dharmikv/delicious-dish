Gourmet Farmers Market & Fairfax Food & Walking Tour

Duration: 3 Hours
Maximum 10 people
Meeting point
7100 Beverly Blvd, Los Angeles, CA 90036
View on map
departure time
at 12:00 PM
What's included
  • Food tastings and non-alcoholic drinks
  • Bottled water
What's not included
  • Pick up from hotel
  • Alcoholic beverages (available for purchase)

Explore the rich tapestry of cultures that have shaped this quintessential LA neighborhood, from its Jewish roots to its modern multicultural identity. Experience the bustling energy of the iconic Original Farmers Market, a beloved Los Angeles landmark since 1934, and discover why it remains a foodie paradise.

This tour features:

  • 7-9 stops at top-rated restaurants and eateries
  • Mix of trendy hotspots, hidden gems and local favorites
  • Tastings at diverse food stalls and gourmet shops in the Original Farmers Market
  • Exploration of Jewish delis, bakeries, and traditional shops along Beverly Blvd and other neighborhood streets
  • Insights into the area's transformation and its significance in LA's culinary scene
  • Stories of immigrant families who built thriving businesses and shaped local food culture
  • Visit to key cultural landmarks, including the famous CBS Television City
  • Opportunity to shop for unique local products and gourmet items

Perfect for food enthusiasts, history buffs, and anyone looking to experience the authentic flavors of Los Angeles.

Cancellations

Customers will receive a full refund or credit with 24 hours notice of cancellation. Customers will also receive a full refund or credit in case of operator cancellation due to weather or other unforeseen circumstances. Contact us by phone to cancel or inquire about a cancellation. No-shows will be charged the full price.

Good to know
  • The tour is conducted rain or shine.
  • We like to blend in with the locals. Dress in comfortable clothing, and leave any flashy or expensive clothes and jewelry at home. Note: Mexico City can be chilly in the mornings and warmer in the afternoon, so it’s best to dress in layers.
  • This tour includes a lot of food. We recommend arriving hungry, and not eating breakfast beforehand.
  • Vegetarians can be accommodated on this tour, but meat and vegetables will be cooked on the same surface.
